# Cheriven Empowerment Foundation Website

## Project Overview
This project is the official website for the Cheriven Empowerment Foundation, a Nigeria-based non-profit, non-religious, non-political organization dedicated to giving hope to the less privileged and vulnerable in society. Founded by kind-hearted individuals, the foundation aims to alleviate poverty and ensure sustainable livelihoods for the poor, focusing on people with disabilities, widows, women in distress, street children, and more. Services include vocational training, skills acquisition, securing scholarships for the needy, and graduate training.

## About Us
Cheriven Empowerment Foundation is committed to improving the lives of the most vulnerable in society. Our organization works tirelessly to provide support and opportunities to those in need.

## Our Mission
We work with communities and individuals to alleviate poverty among rural people, extend charity to the most vulnerable, and empower citizens striving to exercise their human rights and achieve sustainable livelihoods.

## Our Vision
Faithful commitment to peace, social justice, and sustainable development; where all people live together with mutual respect and without recourse to any form of violence to resolve conflict.
